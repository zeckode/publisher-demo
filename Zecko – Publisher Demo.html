Product Description

Whether you like them naturally groomed or strikingly bold, perfecting your brow game is a fine art. With the new SUGAR Arch Arrival Brow Definer, you can now discover the artist in you and score your brow goals in no time. This retractable pencil boasts of a specially-designed triangular slant that gives you both – precise shaping with a pointed tip and generous filling with a flat edge. Your search for your brow weapon of choice ends here – give it a spin today!

Benefits

Bold, bushy, sharp and thin, there’s no brow that the SUGAR Arch Arrival Brow Definer cannot work its magic on. The sweat/humidity-resistant, waterproof formula ensures the colour doesn’t slide around through the course of the day & lasts for 12 hours straight making it your go-to product for keeping your brows on point.

Shade and Pigmentation

As I have medium to dusky complexion (MAC NC-42) I picked up SUGAR Arch Arrival Brow Definer – 01 Jerry Brown which is a dark brown shade. The colour is natural looking and perfect for my skin tone. Since my hair are also brow-black hence it matches with them completely.

Texture, formula and staying power

SUGAR Arch Arrival Brow Definer has a waxy texture, which is perfect to have better control over the product. It doesn’t feel patchy or dry. Initially I felt it was a little more on the dry side, but once I warmed it up a bit using my fingertip, it became smooth, soft and more comfortable. It glided effortlessly. It’s 100% waterproof, transfer proof and does not smudge or run. It has a long wearing formula which stays put at least 8 hours easily on my normal skin. If you have an oily skin then this probably will last less.

I love how well it goes along with Renée Fab Face Nude, which is a 3 in 1 makeup stick with eye shadow, blush and lipstick, enriched with Vitamin E. Try it out!
